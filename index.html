✦ How It Works

Three Simple Steps

From any audio file to immersive 8D audio in under a minute.

1

Pick Your Track

Tap the Studio tab and select any audio file from your device — MP3, WAV, AAC, or M4A. The file picker shows the filename and size instantly.

2

Choose Your Effect

Select from 8D, 3D, or custom spatial settings. Adjust reverb, panning speed, and bass boost to your taste using the live equalizer.

3

Convert & Enjoy

Hit Convert. Watch the real-time progress bar. When done, a success popup appears — tap "View in Library" to find your new track glowing in the library, ready to play.
